## üöÄ How to Use

Embedding the calculator into Notion takes less than 30 seconds.

1.  **Copy the URL:**
    ```
    https://tj-paul.github.io/calculator-notion-widget/
    ```

2.  **Paste in Notion:**
    Go to your Notion page and paste the link.

3.  **Create Embed:**
    A small menu will appear. Click on `Create Embed`.

4.  **Resize & Use:**
    That's it! You can now resize the calculator block to fit your layout.
